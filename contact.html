Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products completely organic?

Yes, all SKB AGRI products are made using organic ingredients and are safe for soil, crops, and the environment.

What makes your products organic?

Our products are made from natural ingredients and do not contain harmful chemicals. We prioritize eco-friendly practices to ensure safety for crops and the environment.

How do SKB AGRI products enhance soil health?

We offer soil conditioners and humic acid that improve soil structure, nutrient availability, and microbial activity, ensuring healthier crops and sustainable yields.

Where can I purchase your products?

Our products are available through our website and authorized retailers across India. Contact us for more information on local availability.

Can I use your products alongside traditional farming?

Yes, our organic solutions can be safely used in conjunction with traditional farming practices, promoting a gradual transition towards sustainable agriculture.
